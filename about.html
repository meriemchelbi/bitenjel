About this site...

I'm a glutton with a budding interest in programming. Through 'Code First: Girls', I've embraced an opportunity for shameless self-promotion by building a gateway site for my Tunisian recipe blog.

Bitenjel - the Tunisian word for 'aubergine' - first saw the light of day as an attempt to capture the recipes I grew up eating, and share the little-known wonders of Tunisian food with others.

As with all traditional cooking, there is no single source of truth, and I have introduced my own personal touches to some of these dishes. In some cases, I've gone as far as coming up with some original ideas using classic Tunisian flavours. Risky strategy, but I've yet to hear any complaints!
